🤖 How the AI Works
1. Camera captures live video frames at 30fps
2. FaceNet model detects and extracts face embeddings (128-dimensional vectors)
3. Embeddings are compared against enrolled students using cosine similarity
4. If similarity > threshold (85%), student is marked Present with timestamp
5. Attendance is saved to database and report is updated in real-time
